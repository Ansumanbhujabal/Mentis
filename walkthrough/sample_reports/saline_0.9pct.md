## Risks & Alternatives

0.9% saline, commonly referred to as normal saline, is a widely used intravenous fluid composed of 9 grams of sodium chloride per liter of water, which exceeds the salt concentration of blood [source](https://www.news-medical.net/news/20220630/Better-safer-treatment-option-than-saline-solution-may-be-available-for-hospital-patients.aspx). Despite its ubiquity in North America, particularly in emergency and perioperative settings, the solution is associated with potential risks, such as metabolic acidosis, acute kidney injury, and increased mortality rates [source](https://www.news-medical.net/news/20220630/Better-safer-treatment-option-than-saline-solution-may-be-available-for-hospital-patients.aspx).

Emerging alternatives, including balanced crystalloid solutions such as lactated Ringer’s and Plasma-Lyte, offer advantages by incorporating electrolytes like potassium and calcium to better mimic the plasma composition. Research from two large trials, SALT-ED and SMART, underscores the marginal but notable benefits of balanced solutions over saline, particularly in reducing complications like hyperchloremic acidosis [source](https://www.upmcphysicianresources.com/news/010318-should-lactated-ringers-become-the-default-intravenous-solution).

Recent challenges to normal saline's dominance include supply chain interruptions. For instance, prefilled saline flush syringes were flagged as a shortage item by the FDA in March 2022, demonstrating the vulnerability of supply channels [source](https://www.drugs.com/fda-alerts/2410-0.html). Additionally, product recalls, such as those involving Nurse Assist LLC in November 2023, due to contamination concerns, highlight risks related to specific manufacturers [source](https://data.nrhp.org/wp-content/uploads/2024/02/FDA-Safety-Communication-03-12-24-Recall-of-Certain-Saline-and-Sterile-Water-Medical-Products-Associated-with-Nurse-Assist.pdf).

Moving forward, procurement strategies should weigh the clinical benefits of balanced solutions, while monitoring regulatory updates and addressing supply chain vulnerabilities proactively.
